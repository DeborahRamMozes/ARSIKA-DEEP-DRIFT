## Project Intent

ARSIKA × Deep Drift explores how:

- layered aesthetic logic,  
- symbolic cosmology, and  
- narrative structuring  

can inform the way we understand synthetic cognition and human–AI co-evolution.

The site documents concepts, not operational systems.
