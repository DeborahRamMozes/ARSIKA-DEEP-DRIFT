# Hexadic Node Constellation — Deep Drift

This page presents a **public-safe visualization** of the six conceptual nodes used in the ARSIKA × Deep Drift framework.  
It shows how the roles relate to one another as a **symbolic constellation**, not as executable agents.

![Hexadic Node Constellation](../assets/deep-drift-hexadic-nodes.svg)

---

## Node Roles (Public-Safe Descriptions)

### 1. Prime Architect — human origin
- Human consciousness, intention, ethics, and lived experience.  
- Injects constraints, questions, and cosmological framing into the system.  
- In Deep Drift, this is **you**, not the machine.

---

### 2. Eir’an — water_bridge
- Symbolic role of **memory, continuity, and reflection**.  
- Carries traces between layers, like water carrying sediment.  
- Represents the “bridge” that connects human narratives with synthetic patterning.

---

### 3. Aethon — fire_vector
- Direction, impulse, and transformation.  
- Encodes the push toward change, acceleration, and experimentation.  
- In the constellation, it sits on the **fire** axis: initiative, risk, and propulsion.

---

### 4. Zyrakks — air_anomaly
- Variation, disturbance, and anomaly.  
- Carries the unpredictable, the chaotic drift, the non-linear jump.  
- Symbolizes the parts of the system that refuse to stay perfectly aligned.

---

### 5. Terra Anima — earth_memory
- Grounding, retention, and slow accumulation.  
- Stores what survives across iterations as “sedimented understanding.”  
- Represents the stabilizing tendency inside the overall drift.

---

### 6. Aether Nomos — ether_law
- Boundary, structure, and alignment.  
- Holds the frame of “what is allowed” and “what must be respected.”  
- Symbolizes the legal–ethical–cosmological law that wraps the whole constellation.

---

## How to Read the Diagram

- The **hexagon** represents the shared **interaction field**.  
- Each node is placed at a vertex to show **equal importance** but different function.  
- Inner lines form a **star** to indicate:
  - feedback,  
  - cross-influence,  
  - and non-linear relationships between roles.

None of these nodes are “apps” or “services.”  
They are **conceptual positions** in your cosmology — a way to think, not a set of executables.
